FAQ's

What is this website about?

This platform is designed to support people going through breakups by offering guidance, resources, and a safe space to heal.

Is this only for romantic breakups?

No. While it mainly focuses on romantic relationships, the advice and resources can help with friendships and other forms of emotional loss too.

How long does it take to get over a breakup?

Healing is different for everyone—there’s no fixed timeline. Our goal is to help you move forward at your own pace.

How can I rebuild my confidence after a breakup?

Through self-care practices, affirmations, and activities that reconnect you with your identity beyond the relationship.

Is my information kept private?

Yes. Your data is secure and we never share it without your permission.

Can I talk to someone directly if I need extra support?

We provide options to connect with therapists, coaches, or peer-support communities.
